LIMO MEEG: the application software for the LInear Modeling of

Magneto- and Electro- Encehalographic Data.

 

What can LIMO MEEG do?

Our toolbox offers a range statistical tools to interface with major human electrophysiology software. It relies on a hierarchical modelling approach: for each subject we model the experimental design and obtain parameters; at the group level do the 'usual' statistics on those parameters. Thuis works fo ERP, power spectrum or ERSP (time/frequency) at the channel level and on IC (from ICA decomposition). 

Supported tests: one sample t-test, two-samples t-tests, paired t-tests, ANOVA, ANCOVA, repeated measures ANOVA, single and multiple regressions.

Additional tests (available but non supported at present): Linear and Quadratic Discriminant Analysis, Spatial MANOVA.
